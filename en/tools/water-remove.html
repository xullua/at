Draining water from phone speakers

When water clogs the speakers of your phone and causes the sound to become muffled, you can efficiently eliminate water from your phone's speakers by playing a 165 Hz sound. Using this site, you can play the sound with significantly less data traffic than playing the sound on YouTube or other sites.

・For iPhone, the bottom speaker is only on the right side.

・In silent mode, sound may not be heard.

Step1：Turn off silent mode and maximize volume.

Step2：Play.

Step3：Wipe water drained from the speaker.

Step4：Check the volume and silent mode.
